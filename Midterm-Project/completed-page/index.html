Employee Highlight: Adya

"Since officially retiring from agent work, I've also been promoted to a position where I finally have an office! It's certainly something to get used to. Since my work generally consists of meeting with civilians and discussing their experiences, I've tried to make it a welcoming space-- less of an office office and more of a space for simple conversation. I like to play those long, continuous playlists of lo-fi music with the cute video loops, especially when I speak to kids. They really seem to enjoy it!"

Now a representative speaker for the American Cybernetics Association, Adya Milana Prisham is a former field operative. Her work with the ACA focuses primarily on outlining ethical and moral governing and production of bionics.
