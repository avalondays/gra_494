A molecule like this (an image, < a> tag, and caption text) can be duplicated and reused for multiple articles or pages on a site. Molecules like this are common on mostly identical, paged-based sites, such as a news website. Beyond the image, link and text, there's also some specific styling; the text drop shadow, the bottom gradient, and the underscore at the end of the text are all CSS.
